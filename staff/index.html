Angel Roden

Loves to teach and play with youth! Former preschool teacher for younger children. Finding and implementing kid activities that will improve learning and behavioral life skills is a passion. Life lights up with joy seeing children find their best selves. Confident that summers can be an amazing experience!

Born and raised in Indiana. A mother of two young girls; ages 3 and 7. The family loves being outside hiking, biking, and canoeing. Values honesty, responsibility, and patience.

Received bachelor’s degree in Psychology with minor in Philosophy from Indiana University-Purdue University. Hobbies include baking, cake decorating, (food) photography, blogging, cycling, event planning and traveling.
